Events!

MIT SAAS works hard to bring you exciting events, study breaks, dances, cultural shows, and more throughout the year! Please check out our facebook page and join MIT SAAS to keep up-to-date on all of our amazing events! Becoming a member is easy: come to any of our events throughout the year and ask us about joining SAAS. Membership grants free access to all of our study breaks and updated information about all of our events throughout the year. Email saas-exec@mit.edu for more information! Annual Events

Check out some info and pictures about our annual events!

Milan: Garba Raas

Every year, we work together to put on our annual Garba Raas event, Milan! This event is one of our most popular events, and it's open to everyone at MIT and the community. Our annual Garba took place on October 6th, 2017 this year. Keep on the lookout for more information about our event next year!

Holi

Holi is the festival of colors, and we work to bring you holi every year! This year, Holi took place in May!

C-Show

MIT SAAS's Culture Show has become a time-honored yearly tradition at MIT, and holds a prominent place on campus as the largest student-run show at MIT. CShow brings together students from all backgrounds from all around campus to participate in a south asian-themed bonanza including everything from traditional dances to Bollywood hits. Please check back in early spring for more details on CSHOW 2018!
