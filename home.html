South Asian Association of Students

Welcome to MIT SAAS!

We are the MIT South Asian Association of students, one of the largest student groups on campus representing Indians, Pakistanis, Bangladeshis, Sri Lankans, Nepalis, and other South Asians at MIT. SAAS strives to foster a sense of community at MIT for all students interested in getting a taste of South Asian culture! Find out more about us, board, and our annual events above, and check out our most recent upcoming event below! Feel free to reach out to us at saasboard@mit.edu with any questions you might have!


Want to publicize your event through us?

Contact us at saas-exec@mit.edu or saasboard@mit.edu to help publicize your event to our members! We hope to foster South Asian culture across campusa and would love to help represent other groups as well.
